## North Star

> LifeOS is David's living operating environment - a single extension of his
> intent that lets him see, direct, and advance his company, personal,
> development, and home life as one coherent whole.

## Immutable Anchors

| ID | Anchor |
|---|---|
| `NS-EXTENSION` | LifeOS is a living extension of David's intent. |
| `NS-WHOLE` | Company, personal, development, and home form one coherent whole. |
| `NS-CLARITY` | David can see and understand the whole. |
| `NS-DIRECTION` | David can direct it as one system. |
| `NS-PROGRESS` | LifeOS advances every domain without fragmenting the whole. |

## Success Test

> LifeOS succeeds when David can understand, direct, and advance any part of
> his life without switching between fragmented systems or losing sight of the
> whole.
