## Contract invariants (apply to every row)

1. **Exactly one canonical owner per durable state class.** Anything else is a derived copy with a declared, directional, idempotent sync edge (POSTGRES-001 gate).
2. **RuVector-or-any-index is derived, never authoritative.** It must be rebuildable from versioned source rows alone.
3. **No dual-write.** A writer writes to the canonical store; replicas/copies are filled only by the declared sync path.
4. **No silent reclassification.** Current durable redb uses (CodeDB source blobs, envctl migration_db) keep durable status until STORE-001 explicitly decides otherwise (PGAUTH-002 gate).
5. **Refuted mechanisms may not justify assignments.** The mmap/OS-cache-bypass and AVX-512 arguments for redb are refuted (REDB-CLAIM-002, REDB-CLAIM-010, EDGE-CLAIM-002); durable-authority arguments must rest on the verified benchmark (`NBVERIFY-001.redb-benchmark.summary.json`) and the parity-tested implementations.
